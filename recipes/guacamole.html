Guacamole

Description

Tasty guacamole that is the hit of a party and will be gone before anyone sits at the table.

Ingredients

  • 5 avocados, peeled, pitted and mashed
  • 2 tablespoons of fresh lemon juice
  • 3/4 cup of minced green onion
  • 1/2 cup of minced fresh cilantro
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Stir together the avocado and lemon juice in a serving bowl; add the green onion and cilantro; mix well.
  2. Season with salt and pepper.
  3. Serve immediately or store covered in refrigerator with avocado pits in the bowl to keep from browning.
