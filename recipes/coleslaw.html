Coleslaw Dressing

Description

A creamy coleslaw dressing. Can be refrigerated or poured immediately to make your meal even tastier.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon vinegar
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Whisk mayonnaise, sugar, lemon juice, vinegar, pepper, and salt together in a bowl until smooth and creamy.
